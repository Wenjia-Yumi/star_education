Yigao Technology Group (一高科技集团) Authorized Channel

As STAR DIGITAL's sole authorized partner in China, Yigao Technology Group channels its domestic and cross-border e-commerce client projects directly into the Industry College. Projects are routed dynamically based on student capability fit and training milestones.

Under the "校中厂" (in-school factory) operating model, the Cloud-Product Hit-Product R&D Lab incubates the Industry College's own branded products. Own-brand operations Cross-border channel ops Innovation & entrepreneurship training

Escalating Progression Model

⚙️

Basic Level (基础工单)

Collaborative Apprentice Stage

Standardized, templated, and highly reusable deliverables. Geared toward students developing initial industry awareness and basic operational competencies.

Typical Examples
  • Single main-image draft
  • Single short-video rough cut
  • Initial influencer outreach emails
💎

Intermediate Level (中级工单)

Independent Apprentice Stage

Requires active professional judgment and creativity. Deliverables must meet clear client acceptance standards under standardized workflows.

Typical Examples
  • Complete main-image delivery pack
  • Finished short-video final cut
  • Full influencer collaboration chain
  • Daily operations of a single store
🏆

Complex Level (高级工单)

Pre-Employee Stage

Multi-stage coordination requiring comprehensive project management, strategic problem solving, and complex client acceptance criteria.

Typical Examples
  • Batch content production for brand matrices
  • Monthly contracted store operations
  • Full-event livestream scripting & execution
  • End-to-end cross-border project delivery
Traceable Platform Operations

The full lifecycle, on one platform

Every step from dispatch to settlement runs on STAR DIGITAL's proprietary teaching & service outsourcing platform — every action timestamped, traceable, and auditable.

Enterprise / 企业端

Company posts order requirements based on real teaching or outsourcing needs — outlining difficulty, guidelines, scope, unit price, and deadlines.

01 Dispatch派单
Student / 学生端

Orders sync in real-time. Apprentices select orders matching their learning stage (collaborative / independent / pre-employee) and capability profiles.

Enterprise / 企业端

Allows group forming. Students may bundle into collaborative teams for medium and high difficulty orders to ensure high completion rate.

02 Accept接单
Student / 学生端

Order acceptances are logged on the platform — storing who accepted, timestamps, committed delivery window, and skill checkpoints.

Enterprise / 企业端

Mentor consultation is provided. Apprentices can communicate with industry mentors via the platform's chat module during designated office hours.

03 Deliver交付
Student / 学生端

Apprentices upload the actual deliverables (designs, videos, scripts, logs, data audits) directly to the platform within the agreed deadline.

Enterprise / 企业端

Enterprise clients initiate first-tier checks on the platform. The enterprise mentor reviews quality, professional norms, and industry standards.

04 Verify验收
Student / 学生端

Second-tier check runs via the order business unit against real-market criteria (STAR DIGITAL × Yigao real chain). Dual verification ensures robust log accountability.

Enterprise / 企业端

Once verified, the dispatch unit price is routed to the settlement queue. System generates statements within 15 working days post natural month.

05 Settle结算
Student / 学生端

Apprentices receive monthly payouts based on the STAR DIGITAL algorithm. They can track cumulative orders, settlements, and GMV contribution metrics.
